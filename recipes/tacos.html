Description

This Mexican dish has been instrumental piece of American dinners for a century. The combination of beef and cheese in a flour shell never fails to make people of all ages fall in love and never look back.

Ingredients

  • hamburger meat
  • shredded cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • red onion
  • flour/corn tortilla shells
  • taco seasoning, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. cook the hamburger meat and season
  2. chop the onion, tomatoes, and lettuce
  3. assemble lettuce, onion, tomatoes, meat, and shredded cheese on your tortilla shell
  4. enjoy!
