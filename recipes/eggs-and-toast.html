Description

The classic American breakfast that needs no introduction. You grew up eating it, not lets make it!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • shredded cheese
  • sliced bread
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. melt a bit of butter in your pan
  2. whisk your eggs together and add salt and pepper to the mix
  3. pour your egg mix into the pan at medium heat
  4. continuously fold your eggs in the pan until cooked to your liking
  5. toast your bread and butter it
  6. place your eggs on your bread
  7. enjoy!
