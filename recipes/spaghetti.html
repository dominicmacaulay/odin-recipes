Description

Spaghetti is one of the classic dishes at the dinner table. It is easy to see why. Blending meat and tomato sauce with spaghetti noodles is easy to make and guarantees happy guests at the table.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • hamburger meat
  • parmesan cheese
  • seasonings of your choice

Steps

  1. bring a pot of water to boil
  2. add spaghetti noodles, stir occasionally
  3. in a large pan, cook your hamburger meat and season to taste
  4. once meat has fully cooked, add tomato sauce and stir
  5. once noodles are ready, strain and rinse
  6. serve and enjoy!
